THROUGHLINE
===========

This folder is a tool that reads your sources (PDFs, EPUBs, photos of
pages), finds connections across them with verified quotations, and
drafts prose for the connections you decide to keep. Everything runs
on your own computer. Nothing you put in ever leaves it.

You only ever need three things in this folder, "Install Throughline",
"Throughline", and this note. There is also an "app" folder that holds
the working parts. You can ignore it completely, do not open it.

FIRST TIME ONLY
---------------
1. Right-click (or hold Control and click) the file called
       Install Throughline
   and choose Open, then Open again. Your Mac asks because the file
   arrived over the internet, that is all. You only do this once.
2. A window walks you through setup by itself. It downloads the AI
   parts, a few gigabytes, so give it 20 to 45 minutes. When it says
   ALL DONE, close it.

EVERY TIME AFTER THAT
---------------------
Double-click
       Throughline
and type numbers. That is the whole interface.
  1 starts a chapter and opens a folder, drop your sources in it.
  2 reads the sources. Slow, an hour for a big stack. Leave it open.
  3 finds connections and opens the report for you.
  In the report, change "Decision: candidate" to "keep" or "drop".
  4 writes drafts for your keeps.
  5 makes a picture of the connections and opens it in your browser.

WHAT TO TRUST
-------------
Every quotation is machine-checked, word for word, against your
sources. A quote it cannot verify is flagged, never presented as
evidence. If a draft ever quotes something that is not in the
evidence, the draft carries a red warning naming it.

Two honest notes. Page numbers refer to PDF pages, so map them
before citing. And this free version's connections are solid but a
notch below what Simon sends you from the assisted version, so think
of this as your reading assistant, and of Simon's rounds as the
polished pass.

If anything confuses you, email Simon. A screenshot of the window
is always enough.
